Concierge

Working as part of the IBM FISE v2 portfolio of projects to help the elderly in times of social isolation through voice assistance.

View on GitHub

About

Concierge is specifically designed with elderly users in mind who, in these turbulent times, struggle to interact with the complexities of the current technological landscape. The service will be primarily available through an app which users will be able to download onto their phones. The app will use speech as its primary mode of communication, allowing users to verbally issue commands and receive audio responses.

Friends of elderly users, called admins, will be able to manage the details of the users - they can add/remove services from their accounts to enhance their experience.
